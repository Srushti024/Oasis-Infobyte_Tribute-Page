SINDHUTAI SAPKAL


1948-2022


Sindhutai Sapkal (14 November 1948 – 4 January 2022) was an Indian social worker and social activist known particularly for her work in raising orphaned children in India.She adopted more than 1000 Orphans children's and gave them a better quality of life.

She was awarded the Padma Shri in 2021 in Social Work category..
